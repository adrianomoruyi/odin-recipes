Mass Gainer Shake

Description

Mass Gainer shakes are perfect if you would like to gain some weight while working in the gym. It packs over 1000 calories into an easy-to-consume package and tastes pretty good too!

Ingredients

  • Whey protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 5g creatine
  • 500ml whole milk
  • 1 cup oats

Steps

  1. Cut your banana and put in the blender with the milk
  2. Add in the peanut butter along with the whey, creatine, and oats
  3. Blend together and enjoy!
